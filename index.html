The chances are, if you’re coming to our wedding, you probably know at least one of us, but just in case you need something to jog your memory…

*** SIMONE ***

Simone: Never happier than when he’s got a dozen projects on the go, Simone spends every free moment tinkering with anything that can be taken apart or that requires him to get out his soldering iron. He’s had his fair share of wacky ideas: the robotic laundry basket, the self-pasting toothbrush, a gadget to relieve you of the tedium of ironing shirts. But apparently all this wasn’t enough to keep him occupied because he has now taken on his most challenging project to date: a lifetime of putting up with Catriona and her infuriating tendency to tidy things away.

*** CATRIONA ***

Catriona: She's profoundly embarrassed by the existence of this website, but recognises that it may be useful to some of you, and hey, it gives Simone another excuse to faff about on his computer. While Simone is crouched over his keyboard, Catriona is most likely to be found, mud splattered and red in the face, on some grim cross-country run fuelled by leftover panettone and podcasts aimed at the over 50s. Either that or she's stuffing her face while reading a novel full of women in bonnets.
