ACCOMMODATION

Below are a few accommodation options for those of you who don't live locally. You can stay with us at Dalhousie itself, book a room at a local hotel or B&B, or opt to stay in Edinburgh.

STAY WITH US AT THE CASTLE

SLEEP NEARBY

Hotels and B&Bs in the area

Premier Inn Dalkeith offers affordable accommodation just 5 minutes by car from Dalhousie and 15 minutes from Edinburgh city centre.

The Retreat Castle Hotel is a family run guest house in Bonnyrigg, less than 1 mile from Dalhousie and about 20 minutes from the centre of Edinburgh.

There are a number of other hotels and B&Bs in the surrounding villages of Dalkeith and Roslin.

STAY IN EDINBURGH

Edinburgh city centre is just 15 minutes from Dalhousie Castle and offers a range of accommodation options, including hotels, B&Bs and Airbnb rooms and apartments.
